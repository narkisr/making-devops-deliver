The sound of BREAKAGE

  • OPS bottleneck
  • Progress/Change is hard
  • Collaboration sucks

Bring back the Dev in Devops

Use source

  • Closest to the truth
  • Self documenting
  • Easy to collaborate

Use best practices

  • Testing, Metrics, Linting
  • Build process and continuous
  • Versions/dependency management

Be consistent

  • Same tools/practices across the board
  • Well defined layout
  • A (sane) naming scheme

Evolve

  • Update regularly (OS, IDE, etc..)
  • Constantly Improve workflows/process
  • Follow online trends

Watch out for

  • Neglected systems
  • Single point of control
  • Proprietary software
  • Lack of backup/security
